Helpful Tips for the Attendees

  • For new volunteers, please first create an account HERE.
  • Make sure to complete all of the prompts on the screen, including the waiver. If you do not complete the Qualification section, you will not be able to sign up for your event.
  • Then use the event link to sign up.

For New Volunteers Who Are Minors (Ages 14–17) — 3-Step Process:

  • First, sign up for an account HERE.
  • Answer all of the questions including signing the waiver.
  • You will be prompted to enter parent information and email so the parent can sign the adult waiver. The minor will not be able to register without this step being completed.
    Note The parent email tends to go to the junk folder — please have them check there.
  • Once the parent signs the waiver, the minor will receive an email confirming this step is complete.
  • At this point they will be able to go to the event link to sign up.

Wilton Collective is a community of local residents dedicated to strengthening connections throughout our town. We believe open communication and engagement are essential to understanding, progress, and a healthy civic life. By bringing together families, residents, and town organizations, we aim to foster relationships across diverse perspectives and create spaces where information is shared, voices are heard, and community involvement is accessible to all.
